Returns and Exchanges - Faith in the Kitchen Pickleball Apparel

MADE TO ORDER, WITH CARE

Faith in the Kitchen products are made to order with intention and care. Every piece is crafted only after you place your order, which is why our returns policy reflects the commitment behind each item.

"We stand behind the craftsmanship of every piece and are committed to delivering quality that reflects our standards."

Refunds

Because our products are made to order, we do not offer refunds for change of mind or incorrect sizing. We strongly encourage you to review our sizing guide carefully before purchase to make sure you order the right fit.

Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or with a production defect, we will gladly arrange a replacement at no cost to you.

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  • Include a photo of the damage or defect
  • We will coordinate a replacement right away

Exchanges

Exchanges for unworn items may be requested within 30 days of delivery.

  • Item must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition
  • Request your exchange within 30 days of delivery
  • Customers are responsible for return shipping on exchange requests

Our Commitment

If something is not right, we want to know. Our goal is to deliver quality that reflects our standards and honors the trust you put in us when you wear the brand.
