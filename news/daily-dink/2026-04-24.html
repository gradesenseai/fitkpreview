FITK Daily Dink - April 24, 2026

Some of the top stories moving in pro pickleball today, from a candid breakdown of what pickleball's top pros actually earn to fresh pro draws dropping for the Veolia Atlanta Pickleball Championships.

  • Pro Pickleball Salaries: What Top Players Actually Earn

    Business Salaries Anna Leigh Waters Ben Johns JOOLA

    The Dink details Zane Navratil's tier-by-tier breakdown of professional pickleball earnings, reporting that Anna Leigh Waters takes home roughly $4 million per year after taxes and Ben Johns pulls in close to $3 million once league contracts, sponsorships, and appearance fees are stacked. The piece highlights a Franklin paddle deal estimated at $10 million over three years for Waters and a lifetime JOOLA deal worth about $1 million annually plus roughly $500,000 in royalties for Johns.

  • Veolia Atlanta Pickleball Championships draws released with Waters' singles streak on the line

    PPA Tour Atlanta Championships Anna Leigh Waters Veolia Peachtree Corners

    The Veolia Atlanta Pickleball Championships presented by Ensure Max Protein start Monday, April 27 at Life Time Peachtree Corners in Peachtree Corners, Georgia, with professional draws now released across all five divisions. Live broadcast coverage on PickleballTV begins with the Round of 64 on Tuesday, April 28 at 10:00 a.m. ET, and the event is the final chance for players to lock in points toward the PPA Tour Finals.
